MMM... YOU SMELL THAT? Food In Italy

Pasta

Pasta, or carbonara specifically, is a Roman dish made from eggs, hard cheese, cured pork and black pepper. It is really well known for its humble ingredients fused together to create this creamy heaven. Of course, there are many other types of pasta for all tastes.

Caccuicco

Italy is also known for their seafood dishes and caccuicco is an amazing Tuscan fish stew flavored with tomatoes, white wine, olive oil, garlic, fresh sage, and dried red chili peppers. It is said that there are five different types of seafood representing the five c's in the name.

Cannoli

This Italian pastry consists of a crispy, doughy shell with a creamy sweet filling, and powdered with sugar. There are many different toppings to choose from, but the most popular choices are chocolate and chopped up pistachio

Pizza

It started as a way for ancient Neapolians to quickly consume inexpensive food, they made a flat bread topped with various ingredients that can be eatened for any meal. Now, it is probably one of the most popular food in the world.

Risotto

Don't let the simplicity of this dish fool you, it is a delicious northern Italian rice dish that is cooked with broth until it reaches a creamy consistency. This is the most common way of cooking rice in Italy and it can be topped with shrimp, chicken scallop and much much more!

Tiramisu

[ˌtiramiˈsu], meaning "pick me up" or "cheer me up" in Italian, is a delicate coffee flavoured dessert. It is made of ladyfinger cookies dipped in coffee, layered with a mixture of eggs, sugar, mascarpone cheese, and vanilla flavour, and served topped with cocoa powder

WALK WALK FASHION BABY Fashion In Italy

Italy is home to the many high end fashion brands like Gucci, Prada, Dolce & Gabbana, and Versace. These brands are considered the height of fashion where only a few could get their hands on it. However, many can experience the beauty of them in Milan, the epicenter of Italian Fashion. Semi-annually Milan holds a runway show exhibiting all the new styles and colors in season.
